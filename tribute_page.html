Jaytee's music journey

  • 2004 - Started making music with stagename as JayTee at fourteen.
  • 2008 - Joined Click Click Boom as Rapper/Singer/Music Producer.
  • 2010 - With famous rapper Lil' Knight created LadyKillah and a website about R&B and Hiphop in Vietnam. Around this time, he also publish his first mini-album, JUST-A-TEE.
  • 2017 - After years of not publising new songs, he came back with hits. The single Da lo yeu em nhieu hit 5 million views on Youtube in one week, currently at 170 million views. He then collabed with ViruSs (a famous streamer) and later Hyomin (T-ara member).
  • 2020 - Joined Rap Viet as a judge. Rap Viet was a show that brought rap to the larger audience in Vietnam. Later in the year, he released two songs Can gi hon and Di ve nha both were collaborations with famous singer and rapper.
  • 2022 - Released the single Forget about her.
